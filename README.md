
# 📊 Amazon Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

---

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze Amazon sales data to evaluate overall business performance across product categories, regions, time periods, and payment methods.

### Problem Statement:

* How is revenue distributed across product categories?
* Which regions generate higher revenue?
* Is sales performance stable across months?
* What payment methods do customers prefer?

### Business Goal:

To build an interactive dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs, track trends, and make data-driven business decisions.

---

### Dataset Description:

The dataset contains transactional sales data including:

* Order ID
* Order Date
* Product Category (Beauty, Books, Electronics, Fashion, Home & Kitchen, Sports)
* Region (Asia, Europe, Middle East, North America)
* Quantity Sold
* Price
* Total Revenue
* Payment Method (Wallet/UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, Cash on Delivery)
* Customer Rating
* Year
* Month Name

The dataset contains approximately **50,000 orders** used for analysis.

## 🧹 Data Cleaning (Python)

The raw dataset was cleaned and prepared before analysis.

### Steps Performed:

* Removed missing/null values
* Removed duplicate records
* Standardized column names
* Converted order date to datetime format
* Ensured correct numeric data types (Quantity, Price, Revenue)
* Created derived columns:

  * Month Name
  * Year

The cleaned dataset (https://github.com/TrishaKumari-coder/Amazon-Sales-Performance-Dashboard/blob/main/amazon_sales_clean.csv) was used for dashboard creation.

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

### Major Observations:

* Total Revenue generated: **32.87M**

# 📊 Detailed Chart Explanation

---

## 1️⃣ Revenue by Category (Bar Chart)

This chart shows total revenue generated by each product category.

### Observation:

* Beauty generates the highest revenue (~5.6M).
* Sports generates the lowest (~5.4M).
* Other categories (Books, Electronics, Fashion, Home & Kitchen) are nearly equal (~5.5M).

### Interpretation:

Revenue is evenly distributed across all categories, indicating no heavy dependency on a single product line.

---

## 2️⃣ Revenue by Region (Horizontal Bar Chart)

This chart compares total revenue across regions.

### Values:

* Middle East: 8.3M
* North America: 8.3M
* Asia: 8.2M
* Europe: 8.1M

### Interpretation:

Regional performance is balanced. No single region dominates sales, showing diversified market presence.

---

## 3️⃣ Monthly Revenue Trend (Line Chart)

This line chart displays revenue month-by-month for selected years.

### Observation:

* Revenue remains between approximately 2.6M–2.8M monthly.
* No major seasonal spikes or sudden drops.

### Interpretation:

Sales demand is consistent throughout the year, reflecting stable business performance.

---

## 4️⃣ Units Sold by Category (Bar Chart)

This chart shows total quantity sold per category.

### Observation:

* Each category sells approximately 25K units.
* Units sold are nearly identical across categories.

### Interpretation:

Customer demand is evenly spread across product types.

---

## 5️⃣ Monthly Orders Trend (Line Chart)

This line chart shows number of orders per month.

### Observation:

* Orders range between approximately 3600–4400 monthly.
* Trend is steady with minor fluctuations.

### Interpretation:

Customer purchasing frequency remains stable across months.

---

## 6️⃣ Orders by Payment Method (Bar Chart)

This chart shows total orders by payment method.

### Values:

* Wallet/UPI: ~10.1K
* Debit Card: ~10.1K
* Credit Card: ~10.0K
* Cash on Delivery: ~9.9K

### Interpretation:

Customers use all payment methods almost equally, with slight preference for digital wallets.
